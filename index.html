Just Like that

I love you as if my heart has gone wild
I love you like my own child,
I love you like a soldier coming back to his wife,
I love you like that
...
I love you and I wish I could love some more
I love you with a heartache, cuts deep to core
For your voice hard to ignore and eyes I adore
I like you like that

Give me one reason to stop

I stand on the edge,
The bus is coming fast.
I could close my eyes
And let it all be past.
But give me one reason to stop
...
In the depths of hope, my soul cries,
In the debts of sorrow, my happiness flies,
These echoes of grief and haunting lullabies.
To give me one reason to stop.

Don't teach me to love

O, don't teach me to love,
For I'm a master of the art of it
...

An oblivious one asks me passing by
“No”, I said, “hormones die with the body”,
All of love ever was, lived only in mind
Underneath the grave though, a tale gets to live,
A tale that speaks of one of only it’s kind
